For fun, I decided to make a book-sharing site. Anyone can add a photo of a book and a quote to remember it by. The type is FF Tisa Web-Pro and Futura PT.

A team of Engineers and I collaborated with Microsoft on a neighborhood app for Windows 8. I attempted to create engaging representations of nearby locations, by grouping together map tiles, photos, and local info.

Before my car died during a camping trip, I used to hate parking in the city. For fun, I experimented with an interface that visually indicates whether or not you can park on a particular street based on available street-cleaning data. The color also adjusts based on if it is day or night.
